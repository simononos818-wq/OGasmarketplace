## Registration Process

### Step 1: Create Your Account (5 minutes)

1. Visit: **https://ogas-nigeria.com** (or use provided link)
2. Click **"Get Started"**
3. Sign in with **Google** (your business email)
4. Select **"I'm a Seller"**
5. Enter your 11-digit **NIN** (National Identification Number)
6. Enter your phone number
7. Allow **location access** when prompted
8. Provide your business address (street, city, state)

**Important:** Your location is automatically captured to help buyers find you. Ensure location services are enabled on your device.

### Step 2: Complete Business Profile (10 minutes)

Fill in:
- Business name (as it appears on license)
- Description (tell customers about your business)
- Operating hours (when you accept orders)
- Delivery radius (how far you deliver)
- Contact phone number

### Step 3: Upload Documents (5 minutes)

Upload clear photos or PDFs of:
- Business registration/CAC
- DPR license
- Business premises (3 photos)
- Fire safety equipment
- Any certifications

### Step 4: Create Your Gas Listing (10 minutes)

Set prices for cylinder sizes you stock:
- 3kg
- 6kg
- 12.5kg
- 25kg
- 50kg

*You only pay for sizes you actually sell*

Set:
- Current stock levels
- Delivery fee (or ‚Ç¶0 for free delivery)
- Delivery/pickup options

### Step 5: Verification (1-3 business days)

Our verification team will:
1. Review your documents
2. Visit your location (may call to schedule)
3. Check safety compliance
4. Approve your account

You'll receive:
- Email notification when approved
- **Verified Seller** badge on your profile
- Training session invitation

## Managing Inventory

**Why It Matters:**
- Avoid disappointing customers

**How to Update Stock:**

## Order Management

### When You Receive an Order

**Notification:**
- You'll receive SMS and app notification
- Order details include:
  * Customer name and phone
  * Cylinder size and quantity
  * Delivery address
  * Payment status
  * Delivery method (delivery/pickup)

**What to Do:**

**Step 1: Accept or Reject (within 10 minutes)**
- ‚úÖ Accept if you have stock and can deliver
- ‚ùå Reject only if out of stock or can't deliver today
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Late responses hurt your rating

**Step 2: Prepare the Order (15-30 minutes)**
- Verify cylinder quality
- Check for leaks or damage
- Load onto vehicle
- Gather receipt/invoice

**Step 3: Contact Customer (optional but recommended)**
- WhatsApp or call to confirm
- Give estimated arrival time
- Ask for any special instructions

**Step 4: Deliver**
- Follow GPS to customer location
- Bring your OGas seller ID badge
- Ensure safe handling
- Collect payment confirmation (already paid online)

**Step 5: Confirm Delivery**
- Mark order as "Delivered" in app
- Payment will be processed to your account

### Delivery Timeline

**Target Times:**
- Accept order: < 10 minutes
- Prepare: 15-30 minutes
- Deliver: 30-60 minutes (depending on distance)
- **Total: < 90 minutes from order to delivery**

**Fast delivery = Better ratings = More orders**

### Handling Issues

**Customer Not Available:**
1. Call/WhatsApp customer
2. Wait 10-15 minutes
3. If no response, contact OGas support
4. Return to shop if necessary

**Wrong Address:**
1. Contact customer to confirm
2. Get correct location
3. Adjust in app
4. Deliver to correct address

**Customer Wants to Cancel:**
1. Check if order already prepared
2. If yes, explain cancellation policy
3. Contact OGas support if dispute
4. Payment may be refunded to customer

**Cylinder Rejected by Customer:**
1. Ask reason (leak, damage, wrong size)
2. If valid, apologize and replace
3. If unfounded, explain and offer inspection
4. Document with photos
5. Contact support if unresolved

## Safety Standards

### Daily Safety Checklist

**Before Opening:**
‚ñ° Check fire extinguisher (accessible, not expired)
‚ñ° Inspect cylinders for damage or leaks
‚ñ° Ensure proper ventilation
‚ñ° Verify first aid kit is stocked
‚ñ° Test safety equipment
‚ñ° Brief staff on safety protocols

**During Operations:**
‚ñ° No smoking or open flames
‚ñ° Keep cylinders upright
‚ñ° Store away from heat sources
‚ñ° Handle with care (no dropping/dragging)
‚ñ° Use proper lifting techniques
‚ñ° Check for leaks before delivery

**Before Delivery:**
‚ñ° Inspect cylinder (dents, rust, expiry)
‚ñ° Soap test for leaks
‚ñ° Ensure valve is closed tight
‚ñ° Secure cylinder in vehicle
‚ñ° Bring safety card/guide for customer

### Customer Safety Education

**Teach Customers:**
- How to check for leaks (soap water test)
- Proper cylinder storage (upright, ventilated area)
- When to call for help (smell gas, hissing sound)
- Never use damaged cylinders
- Regular maintenance checks

**Provide:**
- Safety instruction card with each delivery
- Emergency contact numbers
- Your contact for issues

### Incident Reporting

**If Accident Occurs:**
1. Ensure immediate safety (evacuate if needed)
2. Call emergency services if required (fire: 112)
3. Call OGas support immediately: [SUPPORT_NUMBER]
4. Document with photos
5. File incident report in app
6. DO NOT dispose of evidence
7. Cooperate with investigation

**Types of Incidents to Report:**
- Gas leaks
- Cylinder damage during delivery
- Customer injuries
- Fire incidents
- Near misses
- Customer complaints about safety

## FAQs

**Q: How much does it cost to join OGas?**
A: Nothing! Registration is free. You only pay commission on completed orders (0% first month, 10% after).

**Q: How long does verification take?**
A: Usually 1-3 business days. We'll schedule a visit to your location.

**Q: Can I change my prices?**
A: Yes! Update anytime in your seller dashboard. Consider market rates and costs.

**Q: What if I run out of stock?**
A: Update your inventory to zero immediately. This prevents orders you can't fulfill.

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: 24-48 hours after delivery confirmation. Minimum payout is ‚Ç¶5,000.

**Q: Can I reject orders?**
A: Yes, but frequent rejections hurt your rating. Only reject if truly can't fulfill.

**Q: What about insurance?**
A: You should have your own insurance. We can recommend providers.

**Q: How do I handle customer complaints?**
A: Resolve politely if possible. Contact support for help. We mediate disputes.

**Q: Can I sell other products?**
A: Currently only LPG/cooking gas. More products may be added later.

**Q: What if my DPR license expires?**
A: Renew it! Your account will be suspended if license expires until renewed.
