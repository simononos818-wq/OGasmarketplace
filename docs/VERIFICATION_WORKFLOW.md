## REJECTION REASONS & GUIDANCE

### Common Rejection Reasons

**1. Invalid/Expired DPR License**
- **Reason:** License expired or not recognized
- **Guidance:** Renew license with DPR before reapplying
- **Reapply:** After obtaining valid license

**2. Unsafe Storage Conditions**
- **Reason:** Cylinders stored near heat, poor ventilation
- **Guidance:** Relocate storage, improve ventilation
- **Reapply:** After safety improvements (with photos)

**3. Insufficient Safety Equipment**
- **Reason:** No fire extinguisher or expired
- **Guidance:** Purchase and service fire extinguisher
- **Reapply:** Immediately after acquisition

**4. Business Registration Issues**
- **Reason:** Business not registered or invalid documents
- **Guidance:** Register business with CAC
- **Reapply:** After registration (4-6 weeks)

**5. Location Concerns**
- **Reason:** Prohibited zone, too close to schools/hospitals
- **Guidance:** Relocate business or provide zoning approval
- **Reapply:** After relocation or approval

**6. Failed Background Check**
- **Reason:** Previous violations, complaints, or fraud
- **Guidance:** Resolve outstanding issues
- **Reapply:** Case-by-case evaluation
